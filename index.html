A simple space for steadier thinking

The app is built around guided emotional check-ins, thought reflection, and practical tools inspired by cognitive behavioral therapy and acceptance and commitment therapy.

What it helps you do

Finding Worth is designed to help you pause, untangle what is happening inside, and choose your next step with more intention.

Name what you feel

Use guided check-ins to identify emotions like anxiety, shame, overwhelm, loneliness, anger, and sadness.

Examine the thought

Work through thoughts with structured prompts, cognitive distortion tools, and ACT-style reflection on usefulness.

Choose what matters

Turn insight into one small next action that supports your values instead of staying stuck in rumination.
