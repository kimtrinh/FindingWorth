A simple space for steadier thinking

The app is built around guided emotional check-ins, thought reflection, and practical tools inspired by cognitive behavioral therapy and acceptance and commitment therapy.

What it helps you do

Finding Worth is designed to help you pause, untangle what is happening inside, and choose your next step with more intention.

Name what you feel

Use guided check-ins to identify emotions like anxiety, sadness, anger, overwhelm, loneliness, shame, and guilt.

Examine the thought

Work through thoughts with cognitive distortion tools, usefulness checks, and reflection prompts that slow your mind down.

Choose what matters

Use values and micro-actions to move forward with one small step when a thought is no longer helping you.

A design that matches the app

Soft surfaces, rounded cards, muted colors, serif headlines, and calm structure echo the feel of the app itself.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 8, 2026

Finding Worth is a self-reflection and wellness app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how that information is used, and your choices.

Information the app accesses

With your permission, Finding Worth may access Apple Health data such as sleep and related wellness metrics through HealthKit. The app may also store journal entries, mood check-ins, reflection responses, and settings that you enter directly.

How information is used

Information accessed by the app is used to provide wellness reflection features, help you review patterns, and support your use of the app’s CBT- and ACT-inspired tools.

Data collection

Finding Worth does not collect personal data from the app to a remote server. Health data, journal content, mood entries, and reflection responses remain on your device unless you explicitly choose to share them yourself.

Health data

HealthKit data is used only for health and wellness features within the app and is not used for advertising, marketing, or data sale.

Third-party services

Finding Worth does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or remote tracking services to collect your personal information from the app.

Your choices

  • You can control HealthKit access at any time in the Health app and in iPhone Settings.
  • You can delete app content from within the app or by removing the app from your device.

Children

Finding Worth is intended for general wellness and self-reflection. It is not intended to diagnose or treat medical or psychiatric conditions.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
